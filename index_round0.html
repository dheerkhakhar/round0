The Forest
of Roots

Long ago, the Forest of Roots kept the land in balance. Rivers stayed clean, trees grew strong, and animals lived in harmony. But everything changed when the Heartstone disappeared.

The rivers no longer flow as they once did, the earth has begun to crack, and darkness spreads deeper through the forest each day.

Before vanishing, the last Guardian left behind a final message:

"If you stand before the Forest of Roots today, then fate itself has led you here. Our world is dying. The Heartstone — the soul of this land — has been lost. And without it, balance cannot survive.

Many tried before you. None returned. But the forest still believes in one final hope... you.

Beyond these trees lie Twelve Ancient Trials, created to test wisdom, courage, unity, and strength. Only those worthy of the Heartstone can complete them.

Every choice you make from this moment onward will decide the fate of this world.

Restore the Heartstone... restore the balance... and become the guardians this land has been waiting for."
